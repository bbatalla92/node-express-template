About Us

Linguartech is a booking platform that connects international college students to worldwide short-term academic programs. We select the best of three different types of programs including immersion language learning programs, music and art programs, science and technology programs. We help students no matter who they are and where they come, they all have the same opportunity to reach to worldwide great programs.
